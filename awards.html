Nirvi Arts - Awards

Embracing Excellence

Awards

Nirvi Arts proudly announces its first-ever awards ceremony, celebrating the rich traditions of Folk Singing, the groundbreaking achievements of Debut Filmmakers & Artists, and the inspiring contributions of Social Work Heroes and more. As a platform dedicated to promoting cultural diversity, artistic innovation, and social impact, we are honored to recognize and applaud the remarkable talents and efforts of individuals who are making a difference in their respective fields.

Categories

Celebrating Creativity, Diversity, and Innovation in Art & Culture
Folk Singing Excellence Awards

Nominations are open to folk singers who have made significant contributions to preserving and promoting folk music traditions. Nominees may submit audio or video recordings of their performances along with a brief biography and description of their work.

Debut Filmmakers & Artists Awards

Filmmakers who have made their directorial, screenwriting, or production debut in the past two years are eligible to submit their debut feature films, documentaries, or short films. Submissions will be judged based on criteria such as storytelling, creativity, technical proficiency, and overall impact.

Social Work Heros Awards

Nominations are open to individuals or organizations making a positive impact in their communities through social work, activism, and philanthropy. Nominees may submit testimonials, project descriptions, and supporting materials highlighting their contributions.

Arts & Culture Awards

From visual arts to literature, music to heritage preservation, these awards honor the visionaries, creators, and champions who enrich our lives and inspire us through their creativity.

Join us...

in Celebrating Excellence, Diversity and Impact.

We invite folk singers, filmmakers, social activists, and community leaders to join us in celebrating the spirit of creativity, compassion, and innovation at the Nirvi Arts-Awards. Together, let's honor the storytellers, changemakers, and cultural ambassadors who enrich our lives and inspire positive change in the world. Stay tuned for updates, announcements, and highlights as we come together to recognize and applaud the remarkable achievements of our nominees and winners.
