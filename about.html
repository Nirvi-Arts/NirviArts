About Nirvi Arts

Igniting Cinematic Dreams

Step into a world of cinematic wonders crafted by an NRI visionary. Experience the magic of storytelling like never before!

Where vision meets purpose...

Nirvi Arts stands as a testament to the indomitable spirit of its founder Mr. Hari P Reddy, an NRI entrepreneur whose journey from the corridors of corporate America to the captivating world of cinema is nothing short of inspiring. Driven by a profound passion for storytelling and a relentless pursuit of excellence, our company embodies the intersection of creativity, innovation, and social impact.

Born from the vision of a successful US IT businessman, Nirvi Arts is the brainchild of an individual Mr. Hari P Reddy, who dared to dream beyond boundaries. His journey serves as a beacon of inspiration for aspiring filmmakers, demonstrating that with dedication, perseverance, and a bold vision, anything is possible.

Our Mission

At Nirvi Arts, our mission is twofold: to produce exceptional cinema that captivates, inspires, and entertains audiences worldwide, and to leverage the power of storytelling as a catalyst for social change. Guided by our founder's vision and values, we are committed to creating films that not only entertain but also challenge perceptions, provoke thought, and spark meaningful conversations. Through our work, we aim to illuminate the human experience, foster empathy, and drive positive change in the world.

Our Approach

What sets Nirvi Arts apart is our unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity, and social responsibility. We approach every project with a deep reverence for the craft of filmmaking, infusing each frame with creativity, authenticity, and purpose. Our team of dedicated professionals brings together a wealth of experience, expertise, and passion, ensuring that every film we produce is a testament to our collective commitment to storytelling and social impact.

Commitment to a Social Change

Friendly Welfare Services (FWS), a philanthropic thought...

Beyond his endeavors in the world of business and entertainment, our founder Mr. Hari P Reddy is also deeply committed to making a positive impact on society. He is the driving force behind the NGO, Friendly Welfare Services. This NGO embodies his philanthropic spirit and dedication to social welfare. Through this organization, he works tirelessly to uplift communities, empower individuals, and create a brighter future for generations to come.
